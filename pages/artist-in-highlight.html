Artist in Highlight
The emerging indie scene has uncovered a lot of hidden gems, both in graphic and product design. Artist in Highlight is our weekly design series where we exhibit and interview such home-grown, unconventional designers. We cover an eclectic range of artists and try to touch upon all disciplines of design.  We ask them questions about their creative process and advice they’d give to their younger selves. Through this we hope to give these artists a center stage, that increases the reach of their work and also inspires other upcoming artists.
By : Aditya Sangal | 12 November 2020
Bhattachin
BHATTCHIN (Sachin Bhatt) is an artist from Bangalore, India. He is primarily known for his illustrated record covers and animated videos for musicians across the globe. BHATTCHIN possesses a unique and excruciatingly detailed illustration style that stems from the research he puts into his work and his love for pop culture. His work is an extension of himself, exploring strange moments of life and human interaction. He has created art for Javed Akhtar, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kiefer, DIVINE, Plini and more. BHATTCHIN was recently behind the visual identity for Neon East Fest, an international music festival featuring the likes of Mura Masa, Little Dragon, JBABE, etc.
Gherkinz
Born and raised in the benign coasts of Madras, Sashank Manohar is a musician/ visual artist/ surfer living a double life, between writing/ touring with his band The F16s and crafting brand identities and illustrations through his alter ego, his phantom - Gherkinz
Yash Pradhan
Yash Pradhan is 25 y/o self taught digital artist, illustrator and graphic designer based in Bombay, India.  Doodling, what used to be a hobby of sorts, is now something he has turned into a profession for himself. He studied banking & business as he graduated from Mumbai University but went back to the roots of scribbling but now digitally.    Started off with a self initiated project of creating posters for 365 consecutive days.    He has been creating these artworks for over 3 years now and finds himself constantly inspired by his daily experiences and observations. The nitty gritty of everyday life fascinates him and provides abundant opportunity to be inspired from.      He has worked with clients which include Adidas, Levis, Raymond, Absolut to name a few. Currently Yash is freelancing fulfilling design briefs with different brands & co's.
Sahana
Sahana has spent large chunks of her adult life dabbling in a few different things, but art is among her few constants. Though she has always enjoyed it, and has tried her hands at different mediums, the digital one has sort of changed the way she views the possibilities of what she can make, and that’s been a lot of fin to explore over the last few months. She’s actually an educator by profession, and she wants to work her way upto finding a way to effectively incorporate art, education and psychology , something that she can share with other people this makes sense to.

Raunak Jangid
Raunak Jangid is a Graphic designer from MIT Institute of Design and currently, he is studying in New York. He focuses on creating smart, provocative, and conceptual work. Through the constant exploration of typography, imagery, and illustration, he believes in pushing his boundaries. Passion is the main driving force behind his creativity. Above all, he is constantly curious, willing to take risks while being flexible.
Ishita Poddar
Ishita Poddar is currently in 3rd year studying Graphic Design at MIT Institute of Design, Pune. Her preferred pronouns are she/her/hers. She is an ambivert, a keen observer, a huge foodie, a quite appreciator of music and movies, and love to travel without an itinerary. Her designs are very research oriented and art comes from lightbulb thoughts.

About
Pixelate is a design community-based in Vellore, with an aim to bring design enthusiasts and creatives in VIT under one roof. Our main objective is to internally improve the design standards of our college by providing designers to those who need em. We as a community help new designers understand the design principles, techniques that can help them down the line improve their own designs, and most importantly we are dedicated to creating designs that stand out.
